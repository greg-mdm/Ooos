About Ooos.

Ooo Digital Media Studio is the independent practice of Gregory Tyler Long, a leading expert in the field of creative digital strategy. He holds a Master of Digital Media degree and a Bachelor of Arts in Media, Information, and Technology.

Our studio serves in the Information and Cultural Industries sector, both in Canada and globally. We design and distribute digital products, host interactive experiences, and provide expert resources on digital media and artificial intelligence management.

By partnering with innovators, artists, and entrepreneurs, we promote cross-sector collaboration. We guide digital transformation, support community initiatives, and spearhead trade diversification efforts to enhance global competitiveness.

Design system

Creative Environments

Each project provides an opportunity for meaningful engagement within a distinctive creative environment, centrally guided by the studio's founder and creative director. The Ooos universe promotes participatory design, informed exploration, and clear pathways at decision points. We offer a variety of inclusive digital media experiences, ranging from observation to exploration and active engagement.

Guiding Principles

  • Design responsibly.

    Make risk, system limits, and user choice and freedom visible.

  • Design for diverse mental models.

    Build for the range of ways people think, perceive, and decide.

  • Design to earn trust.

    Transparent disclosures on every artifact, including AI assistance.

Strategic Priorities

    Design for participation

  • Engagement

    Design for interactivity over spectacle

  • Emotion

    Design with empathy to cultivate relationships

  • Behaviour

    Support understanding and decision making

Practice

The system holds together through how the work is made. Three repeated patterns run in sequence on every project.

  1. 01

    Skills architecture

    Reusable modular workflows live as named skills with clear inputs and outputs. Specialist review patterns plug into any project the same way every time.

  2. 02

    Multi-pass review

    Each artifact runs through accessibility check, design polish, consistency sweep, and hierarchy cleanup. Each pass is scoped and recorded so the next reviewer picks up clean state.

  3. 03

    Prompt structuring

    Constrained prompts with explicit scope, iterative refinement in small steps, and consistency enforcement against the system. Human authorship stays visible at every step.

Posture

Public sources only. Persistent disclosure on every artifact. ABSTAIN treated as a first-class outcome. Privacy by design enforced in code, not policy. AI assistance documented line-by-line in a Source Card on every project.
